# Vanilla Bomj Simulator (VBS)

**Vanilla Bomj Simulator** is a student project developed by a small team of three people in their free time from studies. Development took place from **March 13, 2022** to **December 8, 2023**.

## About the Project

The game tells the story of a former engineer who considers himself a brilliant designer and is obsessed with conspiracy theories. He was fired for alcoholism and stealing parts from the factory. Now he plans to build a flying saucer and fly away from people who don't understand him...

### Concept

Initially, the idea came from recreating the character movement mechanics from the first two parts of "Doom", which later evolved into the idea of turning a hurricane arena shooter into a meditative and heavy gameplay, presenting the player primarily with the task of exploring the world, rather than using it as decoration.

> "The real world doesn't need innovation, everyone is just waiting to satisfy their natural needs"

## Visual Style

The game is made in a voxel style. The map has three types of buildings of different heights. The windows of buildings have individual drawings instead of black voids. Also, during the day you can notice less light from windows than at night.
